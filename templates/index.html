Welcome to JLK

Following the funding of several research projects of multiple post-doctoral students in Africa by the Volkswagen Foundation, the IPM and agroecology project has also been carried out so far. As agriculture is the flagship activity of the continent, this ambitious project, targeting mainly sub-Saharan Africa, has as main objectives: .

  • To build an all-inclusive portal for supporting the IPM technologies deployment in Africa.
  • To develop a portal for IPM technology transfer and outreach.
  • To Establish an electronic technology gateway, which is a one-stop-shop to store, browse, search, query, and visualize IPM technologies.
  • To link farmers to inputs and outputs – products and services.
  • To provide a stand-alone gateway with all African countries boundaries and queries for identifying similar agroecology within the continent.
